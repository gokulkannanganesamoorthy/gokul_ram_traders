Brand Voice

"Trusted by contractors.
Built for industry."

GOKULRAM ELECTRICALS speaks with authority and brevity. No fluff, no retail language. Every word earns its place.

✓ DO
  • Use short, confident statements
  • Lead with quality and trust signals
  • Name specific brands and specs
  • Speak to contractors, not consumers
✗ DON'T
  • Use exclamation marks or emojis
  • Use retail/consumer language
  • Use the logo on busy backgrounds
  • Modify logo colors or proportions
